My Hero: Selena Gomez

“You are not defined by a photo, a like, or a comment.” — Selena Gomez

Selena Gomez is my hero because she represents strength, resilience, and authenticity. She has faced public criticism, health struggles, and intense scrutiny, yet continues to grow and remain true to herself. Her reminder that my value is not determined by others inspires me as a young Black woman in a male-dominated field. She shows me that vulnerability is powerful and authenticity is strength.
